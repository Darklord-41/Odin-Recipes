Omlette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter or Oil
  • Optional: Chopped Vegetables (onions, tomatoes, bell peppers)
  • Optional: Grated Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and whisk them with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Let it cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
  5. Gently lift the edges and tilt the pan to let the uncooked egg flow underneath.
  6. If using vegetables, add them now and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. If using cheese, sprinkle it on top and let it melt.
  8. Stir well and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Omlette!
