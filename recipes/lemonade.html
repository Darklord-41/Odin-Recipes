Lemonade Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups of Water
  • 1 Cup of Fresh Lemon Juice (about 4-6 lemons)
  • 1/2 to 3/4 Cup of Sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Optional: Lemon Slices and Mint Leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice and the remaining 3 cups of cold water.
  3. Add the cooled sugar syrup to the lemon water mixture. Stir well to combine.
  4. Taste the lemonade and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar syrup if needed.
  5. Add ice cubes to the pitcher or directly into serving glasses.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves if desired.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Enjoy your refreshing Lemonade!
