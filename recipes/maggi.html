Maggi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack of Maggi Noodles
  • 2 Cups of Water
  • Optional: Vegetables (carrots, peas, etc.)
  • Optional: Eggs or Chicken

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Add the tastemaker (spice mix) that comes with the Maggi pack.
  4. If using vegetables, add them now and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Cook until the noodles are soft and most of the water is absorbed (about 2-3 minutes).
  6. If desired, you can add a beaten egg or cooked chicken for extra protein.
  7. Stir well and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Maggi!
